I'm Interested!

Informational meeting will be held at the Inverness Airport on April 16, 2022 from 9:00 am to 11:00 am for those who would like to learn more.

  • What is EAA?
    • EAA is the Experimental Aircraft Association and is a community built around avaition.
  • Do I need a pilot license?
    • No, all people interested in avation are welcome.
  • Why should I join?
    • EAA offers a substantial amount of avaition content and the local chapter is a great way to network with other people interested in aviation.
  • What types of activites are planned?
    • The EAA Inverness Chapter plans to have monthly meetings with avaition presenentations and opportunites to socialize with other members.
    • Young Eagles - Focused on ages 8-17 to experience avaition through work shops and local flights.
    • VMC Club - Discussions around visual meteorological conditions flying.
    • IMC Club - Discussions around instrument meteorological conditions flying.
  • How much does it cost?
    • All members must have an active National EAA Membership and is currently $34 for a single membership for one year when selecting auto renew.
    • The Inverness chapter is targeting $30 per year for single membership.
